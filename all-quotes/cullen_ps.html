My Extra-Curricular Interests

My biggest hobby is definitely cars - I love everything to do with cars including actual driving, modifying cars, reading about car news, going to car shows, car photography and so on. I really enjoy learning about and understanding how a car works as a system of mechanical components, for example, the entirety of how a turbocharger works versus a supercharger, and the pros/cons of both. I find myself almost ‘absorbed’ into the whole car world, and I’ve made most of my closest connections through the common hobby. A cool web-based site that I’ve thought about is a Facebook-equivalent focused for the car community. People who are interested in cars tend to be out in public with friends either cruising, hanging out in a parking lots or something similar, and a widely-popular thing to do is attend a car ‘meet’ or unofficial show at a large parking lot/garage. A lot of the time, these events can be a huge success but only if properly created and managed, which is typically by large Facebook groups. The events therefore become a big thing; they require permits, approvals and police detail, and all of this costs money. I think an app that could act as a scheduling system for ‘casual’ car meets and safe locations to do so would be cool. That way people can create the events in a casual manner at no cost, and most importantly, on approved property.
